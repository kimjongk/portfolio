Our Services

Let's go on an adventure.

Mooncolony offers a wide range of art outsourcing services including concept art, illustration, character design, marketing art and art direction. Our versatile team's extensive experience and commitment to excellence will ensure that your project reaches our high standards and allows you to connect with your audience through effective visual storytelling.

From concept to completion, we provide outstanding project management with an emphasis on collaboration and communication. Artists are handpicked for each project, with an Art Director and Producer taking ownership of the creative and project management responsibilities, building a team that fits seamlessly within your production pipeline.

Got the taste for adventure? Discover more about what we can do below.

  • Transport yourself to another world with our concept art services creating memorable characters, epic environments and impactful props.

    Effective concept art assembles ideas into a visual showcase to inspire product development. Using the latest intergalactic technology at our disposal, we turn your brainwaves into world-building concepts that are ready to jump off the page.

    As a game developer, you want to powerfully communicate the epic potential of your project by sparking curiosity and excitement. That’s where we come in.

    As part of this process, concepts can embody an existing distinctive style by following a precise look and feel or we can explore new visual possibilities with first-class art direction. No matter the level of creative direction required, we continuously pursue an outstanding degree of imagination, quality and design. So, are you ready to transport yourself to another world?

  • Make your IP stand out with impactful art direction.

    Art direction establishes a project's overall aesthetics and mood, a visual identity that perfectly aligns with the goals and expectations of the project. Once established, art direction ensures style consistency, precise implementation of artistic vision, and the highest quality delivery of assets.

    An experienced and passionate art director supervises each project we take on from start to completion. Art direction is also offered as a standalone consultation service, providing visual direction and guidance to achieve a unique, effective art style for your project. As part of this service, we will also develop an art bible to compliment your project. This art bible will equip you and your team with a set of clear artistic guidelines to follow, ensuring consistent implementation of the art direction, even when we’re away saving Earth from another alien invasion.

  • Since Mooncolony’s inception, our clients have trusted us to design remarkable and memorable characters across a variety of genres, game styles, and platforms. That's why we’re confident in delivering beautiful results, no matter if it's casual, stylised, cartoon or realistic.

    Our extensive experience and in-depth understanding of 2D character design allows us to create characters with complexity. We don't just create beautiful designs – we go the extra lightyear by taking into consideration the wider context: their backstories, relationships, personalities, and more. You can have complete confidence that our characters will knock your space boots off.

  • Digital illustration is the bread to our butter, the fuel to our rocket, the sun to our stars, the–. You get the point.

    We love telling stories through dynamic illustrations, and creating versatile artwork that makes an impact. We collaborate with game studios on an extensive range of styles and genres to create card art, splash art, and illustrations for cinematic animations or promotional material.

    Get ready to explore the endless potential of illustration and let’s create something truly cosmic.

  • Captivate your audience with striking marketing art. Turn heads as you promote your IP and stake a solid claim in today’s crowded gaming market. Our marketing art services are intended to help you generate buzz before your game hits the shelves (be that a physical or digital shelf). We create all marketing art, including key art and splash art, with flexibility in mind so that it can easily be utilised across a variety of platforms, from billboards to web banners.

    Elevate your audience’s expectations and wrestle attention away from the competition with marketing art that packs a punch!

  • As the way we communicate continues to evolve, the significance of emojis, icons and avatars remains ever-present. You can show gratitude to a teammate with a 🙏 or tell someone they’re playing like 💩. You can see why emotes are integrated across many video game interfaces (UI).

    At Mooncolony, we create fun and eye-catching illustrated emotes that show off personality and emotion for that ✨ perfect ✨ reaction. Say a lot, with a little.

  • Some of our best work has been collaborating with game studios to create animated 2.5D cinematics. 2.5D sits between 2D and 3D animation, bringing an extra dimension to flat images. These are usually large-scale projects that have many moving parts, requiring clients to have a reliable and effective art outsourcing team.

    As that team, our goal is to provide you with world-class solutions at every stage of the process to create complex, layered illustrations optimised for animation. Together, let’s break the mould and tell compelling stories utilising high-end visuals.
